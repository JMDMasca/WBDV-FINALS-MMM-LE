About Us

Digital Horizon Digital Horizon is the leading platform for Vocaloid music discovery and community building. We connect fans, artists, and composers in the vibrant world of virtual singers. Founded in 2025, we've grown to become the premier destination for Vocaloid enthusiasts worldwide, hosting millions of songs, concerts, and creative works. Our Mission To preserve, celebrate, and advance the art of Vocaloid music by providing the most comprehensive archive and the best tools for discovery, creation, and community engagement.
